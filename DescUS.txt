Supports the creation and extension of settlements by independently fulfilling construction orders (command menu key Special 2).

His greatest advantige is that he can transport objects automaticly. The transport start and target can be set in the context menu. He can also supports buildings with energy.

He is a specialist if it comes to construct a building. When the components for the buildings are missing he will produce them.
